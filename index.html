F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does WebCrafters provide?

We provide website design, WordPress development, UI/UX design, website optimization, and maintenance services tailored for startups, businesses, and entrepreneurs.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the project complexity. Typically, a basic website takes 1-2 weeks, a business website 3-4 weeks, and an advanced custom website 4-6 weeks.

Do you provide website maintenance and support?

Yes, we offer ongoing website maintenance, performance optimization, security updates, and technical support to ensure your site runs smoothly.

Can I update my website myself after it's built?

Absolutely! We use WordPress for most projects, which allows you to easily manage content, images, and pages. We also provide guidance and documentation for easy updates.

Do you offer SEO and digital marketing services?

Yes, we provide basic SEO optimization as part of our packages, and we can also create advanced SEO, marketing, and performance strategies to help your business grow online.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing plans starting from basic packages to advanced custom development. Please check our Pricing section or contact us for a personalized quote.
